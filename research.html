Simon Munzert

political science. surveys. data. web scraping. and all that.

Research

Over the past five years, I have pursued two connected research agendas: first, the measurement of public opinion in subpopulations using surveys and auxiliary data sources, second the use survey methodology to increase the value of Internet-based data sources about human behavior and preferences for the social sciences.

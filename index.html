Welcome!

You've reached Simon Munzert's homepage. I'm a Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Politics and Public Administration, University of Konstanz.

I'm a research and teaching assistant at the Chair for Survey Research at the University of Konstanz, Germany. Currently I'm working on my PhD that deals with issues of public opinion measurement and election forecasting. If you want to know more about me, have a look at my CV.

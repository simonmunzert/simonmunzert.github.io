Welcome!

You've reached Simon Munzert's homepage. I'm a postdoctoral fellow at the Department of Politics and Public Administration, University of Konstanz.

I'm working at the Chair for Survey Research at the University of Konstanz, Germany. Recently, I finished my PhD. My thesis deals with issues of public opinion measurement and election forecasting. If you want to know more about me, have a look at my CV.
